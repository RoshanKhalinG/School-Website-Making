About Us

Welcome !

Shree Kanku Secondary School, proudly standing as one of the oldest educational institutions in the region, has been a cornerstone of educational excellence for several decades. Established as a humble primary school in 1959, the institution expanded its academic offerings to the secondary level in 2014. Since then, Shree Kanku Secondary School has been a pillar of educational progress, imparting knowledge and life skills to countless students.

At the core of our institution are dedicated and talented faculty and staff who are committed to creating a holistic learning environment for our more than 350 students. They not only prioritize academic excellence but also nurture values, character, and a strong sense of community responsibility.
